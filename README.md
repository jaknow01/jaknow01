### 2. Academic Projects
Projects and assignments completed during my university studies.

> **Master Thesis (Work in Progress)**
> *This repository is currently private until the completion and defense of the thesis.*
> 
> **Description:** My thesis evaluates the reliability and cost-effectiveness trade-offs of deep learning architectures (2D CNNs, 3D CNNs, Video Transformers) for breast cancer detection in ultrasound videos, aiming to assist diagnosticians amid growing specialist shortages. It uses the BLUVD dataset and explores data augmentation techniques, including synthetic data generation, to assess whether a small, single-institution-scale dataset can yield clinically useful results.
